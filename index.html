About Me

I grew up outside of Atlanta, GA and moved to Nashville to work in recording studios. I did that for a few years and it was a lot of fun. I worked on some cool records and made a lot of great friends. For a lot of reasons I transitioned into AVL systems integration. I helped design and install sound systems, broadcast video systems, room control systems and theatrical lighting systems. Clients ranged from a tiny church in the middle of Indiana corn fields to the Grand Ole Opry House. I learned a ton about process, communication and how teams interact. It was during this time that I got my CSM I Scrum Master Certification. I am now starting a new chapter in my professional career as a software developer. It's a job that I thought I could never have because I didn't go to MIT or build my first computer when I was 3. But over time I realized that development is primarily about solving problems and my time in studios and integration taught me loads about problem solving. I am extremely excited to synthesize my years of experience in other domains with my developing talents in programming.

Education

Nashville Software School

Full Stack Web Development • January 2021

NSS is non-profit, community focused school offering several programs to help prepare students for immediate placement into the nashville software development community. In the full-stack web development bootcamp I learned how to buid single page apps using tools such as: HTML5/CSS3, Javascript, and React on the client-side. For server side tools we started off by using JSON server and then moved to Python and Django. The fundamentals of DRY and SOLID coding were demonstrated and asked of us on a daily basis. We also learned command line and Git. Scrum and Agile principals were taught on several group projects which required to do several of the Scrum events such as Daily Scrums, Sprint Planning Sessions, Reviews and Retrospective meetings. There is a high emphasis placed on "learning how to learn" and not just patterns and templates.

Work

Clair Solutions

System Desiner • January 2019 - Present

As a Systems Desiner at Clair, I was repsonible for selecting all the parts and pieces that went into a client's system. So if they were installing an audio,video, lighting and acoustics systems, it was my role to make sure all the necessary equipment to make everything work was in a contract, and that labor was priced accordingly. Mankin Media Systems

Installer, System Engineer • March 2014 - October 2018

Mankin Media gave me my introduction into the Systems Integration industry. I spent the first year and a half of my time their in a warehouse or on a jobsite building PA systems, wiring racks, mounting TV's, etc. When the weeks of traveling started to wear on me, I transitioned into being a system engineer. In this role I worked remotely several days a week drawing system diagrams in AutoCAD. I would also model sound systems in manufacturer's proprietary software. This is where I became a scrum master and helped facilitate sprints for a development team of four people. Scrum was transformational to how our department and the business ran. After about six months of using the scrum framework, we had cleared our backlog of project tasks and were often working on overcoming technical debt.
